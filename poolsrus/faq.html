Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I select the right pool builder?
    Building a new pool can be a big, overwhelming project. When selecting a pool construction company, make sure to interview your prospective builder very carefully. After getting a list of postential builder in your area, begin narrowing them down. Check your list against the Better Business Bureau , paying attention how long they have beein in business and any complaints or issues associated with the company.
  • Will a pool increase the value of my home?
    Yes. Most studies show that a pool will increase the value of your home anywhere betweeh 7% and 15%, depending on where your house is located. It will also continue to increase in value through your years, just like your home.
  • I've heard that having a swimming pool can be a lot of work. How much work is involved in owning and caring for a swimming pool?

    In the past, pool maintenance was more of a chore. However, with today's technology and conveniences, owning a swimming pool is easier than owning a car! Pool chemical testing and maintenance, cleaning, and preprogrammed filtration and heating can all be automated. Automatic pool cleaners and various sanitizing devices also make your pool simple to maintain.

    For your convenience, Pools 'R' Us offers a monthly maintenance package that makes it even easier to keep your pool clean and ready for fun! Ask a representative today for more details.

  • I have a small and difficult yard to work with. Can I still have a pool?
    Expert builders, such as Pools 'R' Us, can construct quality pools in yards all shapes and sizes. Our expert design and engineering team can work with the most challenging properties, giving you your dream pool. Our innovative and skilled staff can turn your dreams into a beautiful bakcyard resort. Before you you give up on your impossibly small yard or hillside location, call us for design advice and a free consultation. It's free!
  • How should I decide on the design of my pool?
    Each property has unique and different features that shoudl be considered when your pool is being designed. At your request, a design expert from Pools 'R' Us will meet with your at your home to discuss your pool needs an desires. They will thn prepare a custom design, one that fits your needs and your budget.
  • What factors affect the cost of my swimming pool?

    Prices can be affected slightly be factors such as: ease of access onto your property, demolition requirements prior to construction, and local permit costs. Other facts include the size and design of your dream pool.

    Furthermore, much of the variation in price is based on "hardscape", or what is place around your pool to enhace its design. As a customer, you have the greatest influence on your total budget as your control and design of your pool's hardscape.
